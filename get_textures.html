◈ Texture fetcher (browser edition)

Downloads real NASA/USGS/ESA/JAXA global maps. Earth and Moon use fixed 2048×1024 NASA sources (Visible Earth Blue Marble and the Moon Trek LRO WAC global mosaic). Other sources are the Celestia project content pack (GitHub) and Wikimedia Commons mission mosaics. For each body the best match is picked automatically - click any thumbnail in the Alternatives column to swap it if the automatic pick looks wrong (dimensions are shown; prefer maps close to 2:1). Use the search box under the thumbnails to look for something more specific, or the dashed box to keep the current/gradient look for that body.

Steps: 1) Fetch; 2) review previews, swap any bad picks; 3) Download and save over js/textures-data.js; 4) reload the app.
