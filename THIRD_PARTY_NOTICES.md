## Vallado SGP4/SDP4 reference

The implementation and its near-Earth/deep-space verification vectors follow
David A. Vallado, Paul Crawford, Richard Hujsak, and T. S. Kelso,
"Revisiting Spacetrack Report #3," AIAA 2006-6753. See the
[CelesTrak reference publication](https://celestrak.org/publications/AIAA/2006-6753/).
The citation identifies the algorithmic reference and public validation data;
